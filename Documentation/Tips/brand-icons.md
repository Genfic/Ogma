## Notes

* Google
	* Logo should always be on a white or black background
    * Always in the colorful version, never monochrome
* Discord
	* `#5865f2` is "blurple", `#e0e3ff` is "light blurple"
    * Logo can be:
      * Blurple on black
      * Blurple on light blurple
      * Light blurple on blurple
      * Black on blurple
